LLVM Opaque Pointer Support has landed

Crystal 1.8, the most recent release, will support LLVM’s opaque pointers for the first time, allowing the compiler to be built with LLVM 15 or above. Additionally, this update brings a significant improvement to compilation times.Learn all about this change and what it brings to you here, LLVM opaque pointer support has landed - The Crystal Programming Language.
